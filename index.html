Hi, I’m Alexander Bell. I became a web developer with patience, practice and perseverance. I got my start in Southern California and took up web development early in high school. I was immediately fascinated. The idea that you can take literally nothing – a virtual file on a hard drive – and turn it into something meaningful, something beautiful, captivated me.

I spent the next several years learning about web development and all the specifications that went along with it. My websites went from simple table-based layouts to complex div and CSS-based layouts. Over this period, I collected numerous pieces of information about best practices, cross-browser compliance, and accessibility in web design.

While learning HTML and CSS, I also started teaching myself how to use image editing software (Photoshop). I discovered how important it is for a web designer to be able to create and manipulate images. You can’t be that original if you have to rely on other people’s images!

I then decided it was time for me to have my own, personal website. Looking into it, I picked HTML5 and CSS3 to fulfill my needs. I then hand-coded a cutting-edge website to showcase my work, and to share the knowledge I have gained over the years. Now you can see what Alexander Bell is all about as a web developer.
